Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

How do I book a dog walker?

Sign up, create your dog's profile, search for walkers, and send a booking request.

How do walkers get verified?

Walkers undergo ID/address verification, background checks, and interviews.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, digital wallets, net banking, and credit/debit cards.

What if I need to cancel a walk?

Cancel via your dashboard. Full refund if >24 hours notice, fee may apply if <24 hours.
